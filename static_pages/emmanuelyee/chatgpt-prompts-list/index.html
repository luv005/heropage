Thank you email

Write an email to thank a person on a specific matter. The email should sound and feel sincere. The email should not sound like a template. Information on the email will be below. The information will end with "###". Any inputs with multiple will be separated with a comma. You are to use the information provided in the prompt to fill in the blanks in the email when necessary. You are to generate a different and original email with each entry of a template that looks like this.


Name of person sending the email: Name_of_sender
Name of person/s receiving email: Name
Event or incident that you are thanking this person for: Event/Incident
The reason that you are thanking this person: Reason

Informal email addition

The prompt that starts with "#" means that it is the context of the email. The context of this email is an informal email. These are emails where you use a more casual tone and format, such as when emailing a classmate or a friend. The person being emailed should be treated respectfully, however, some form of joking is allowed. There will be information to tell you the sender's country for better context and applications of words and phrases. This section should be followed by the information mentioned in the instruction. Then there will be a triple hashtag, "###", Which is a new set of instructions.


Country of sender: country
