How can a streamer share their gaming setup details?

As a streamer, you can efficiently share your gaming setup details on Hero using our pre-structured space template, Streamer Setups. Here's how to go about it:

First, create your own personalized Hero Page where you'll share everything about your gaming journey. After setting up your Hero Page, generate a new space titled Streamer Setups dedicated solely to showcasing your gaming gear and tips.

The template comes with existing lists which include Tech Gear, Gaming Equipments, Streaming Software, Setup Tours, Recommended Products, Budget Setups, Professional Setups, and Advice for Aspiring Streamers. You can use these categories to populate with your own items.

For instance, in the Tech Gear list, you might list out the specific gear you use, such as your webcam, microphone, and lighting setup. Each of these items should have a mandatory title, but you can further enrich them with images, videos, or expand on their details in a Markdown-formatted note.

You can also create dedicated spaces for setup tours, sharing either photos or video links that give an in-depth view of your gaming workspace. Additionally, you can share a list of recommended products, where you might populate items of the gear you personally love and recommend.

Once you've detailed your setup, you can easily share your space via the share link or the QR code. You also have the ability to let other users bookmark your items, thereby spreading your gaming wisdom amongst your followers.

By creating and sharing your Streamer Setups space on Hero, you're not just promoting your streaming journey, but you're also influencing and building a community by helping aspiring streamers.
