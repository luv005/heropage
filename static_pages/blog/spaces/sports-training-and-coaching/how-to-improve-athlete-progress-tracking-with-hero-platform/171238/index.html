How can I track athlete performance over time?

On the Hero platform, you can easily track athlete performance over time using the Athlete Performance Tracking Space. This Space is already equipped with distinct lists such as Performance Progression, Achievements, Area-wise Performance, Documented Improvements, Monthly Record and Trainee Discussions. It's designed specifically to help you monitor and document the progression of athletes.

You can add items to the Performance Progression list, detailing statistics, metrics, and important points about each athlete's performance over time. Important victories, records broken, or personal achievements can go under the Achievements list. Area-wise Performance lets you break down performance stats into specific areas or skills, while Documented Improvements can track how an athlete has improved in various aspects.

Athlete Performance Tracking also has a Monthly Record where you can track trends and changes in performance on a monthly basis. If you're working with newer athletes, Trainee Discussions allows you to track noteworthy events, observations, or key improvements for each trainee.
