What is Hero, and how does it work?

Hero is an all-in-one link-sharing platform that offers a wide range of features to help users create, organize, and share their content with others. It enables you to:

1. Create and save Spaces:

Hero allows you to create Spaces, which are lists of links related to specific topics or themes. You can save Spaces to check back on later and easily share them with others.
Each Space can be customized to fit your specific needs, making it easy to keep track of various types of content and share them with your friends, family, or audience.

2. Clone Spaces:

With Hero, you can clone Spaces and make them your own, giving you complete control over the content and layout.
This feature makes it easy to collaborate with others or tailor a Space to your unique needs without starting from scratch.

3. Share your profile:

Your Hero profile is your personal "Hero Page," where you can showcase all of the Spaces you've made public.
By sharing your profile, others can easily access all of the content you've chosen to share in one convenient location.
In the following sections, we'll explore the many ways Hero can help you transform how you share links, whether for personal use or to engage with a wider audience. Whether it is to share your content or organize all your links, we will explain how Hero can be your new best friend!

Exploring different ways to share links online

There are numerous ways to share links online, each with advantages and challenges. In this section, we will discuss various ways to share links and the scenarios in which they are most suitable.

1. Sharing links via email

Email is one of the most common methods for sharing links. Whether you're sharing an interesting article, a helpful resource, or a funny video, simply paste the link into the body of an email and send it to your recipient(s).
You can share your spaces by clicking "share" on the top right of the space editor. Why is it important to share your links and spaces in an email?
- Personalization: You can customize the email message, making it more personal and engaging.
- Wide reach: Emails can be sent to multiple recipients at once, allowing you to share the link with a large group of people.
- Compatibility: Most email clients support links, ensuring your recipients can access the content without issues.

2. Sharing links on social media

Social media platforms such as Facebook, Twitter, and LinkedIn are popular places to share links. These platforms make sharing content with your friends, followers, or professional network easy.
- Visibility: Sharing a link on social media can increase its visibility and reach a larger audience.
- Engagement: Social media platforms often encourage engagement through likes, comments, and shares.
- Ease of use: Many websites have built-in social sharing buttons, making sharing a link directly to your preferred platform simple.

3. Using link-sharing apps like Hero

Link-sharing apps like Hero provide a dedicated platform for organizing and sharing links with others. You can create custom Spaces, add links, and share them with a unique URL.
- Organization: Link-sharing apps allow you to categorize and organize links, making it easier for your audience to navigate and find relevant content.
- Customization: With Hero, you can customize the appearance of your Space, enhancing its visual appeal and providing additional context for the shared links.
- Versatility: You can share a single link or an entire collection of links with just one URL, offering flexibility in how you distribute content.
By understanding the various methods available for sharing links online, you can choose the most appropriate option for your specific needs and ensure your content reaches the right audience.

Share your lists with the right audience

To make the most out of your link-sharing efforts, it's important to share your content with the right audience. With Hero, you can share your curated lists with specific people or groups, ensuring that the content you share is relevant and valuable to them. Here's how to effectively share your links:

1. Identify your target audience:

Determine who would benefit most from the content you're sharing. Is it your friends, family, colleagues, or a specific online community?

2. Choose the right platform:

Depending on your target audience, you may want to share your links on social media platforms, forums, or other online communities. Hero's sharing options make it easy to share your lists on various platforms.

3. Use engaging captions and descriptions:

When you share your links, providing context and explaining why your content is valuable is essential. This will increase the likelihood that people will click on and engage with your links.

4. Share at the right time:

Timing is crucial when it comes to sharing content online. Consider when your target audience is most likely to be active and engaged and share your links accordingly.

5. Monitor engagement and adjust:

Keep an eye on how well your shared links are performing. If you notice that certain content isn't resonating with your audience, consider adjusting your sharing strategy or refining your target audience.
By following these steps, you can ensure that your content is reaching the right people and making an impact. Hero makes sharing links with the right audience easy, helping you get the most out of your link-sharing efforts.
