What is a List Maker App?

A list maker app is an online tool that helps you create, organize, and store lists. Hero is the best list maker online, offering a fast and easy way to create and manage lists. With Hero, you can easily add items, save links, and share lists with friends and family.
With its intuitive user interface, you can quickly and easily create and manage lists. Hero makes it easy to keep track of your lists, save time, and stay organized.

How To Make Lists Online

Making lists online can be a great way to stay organized, but with so many list maker apps, it can be hard to decide which one to use. Hero is one of the best list maker apps available and is very user-friendly. Here's how to make lists with Hero:

1. Create your list.

Start by creating a new list. You can give it a title and start adding items to it. You can create as many lists as you want and choose whether to make them public or private.

2. Add items to your list.

Once you've created your list, you can start adding items to it. You can add links, text, images, and more. You can also add notes to items, so you can easily find them later using search.

3. Organize your list.

Hero makes it easy to organize your lists. You can sort items by type, priority, or any other criteria. You can also create folders or sub-lists to keep your lists organized.

4. Share your list.

Once you're done creating your list, you can share it with others. You can share lists with friends, family, or colleagues, or you can make them public and share them on social media.
With Hero's easy-to-use features, you can make lists quickly and easily. Whether you need to make grocery, to-do, or project lists, Hero is the perfect list maker app.

How To Share Lists Online

Sharing lists online can be a hassle, but it’s easier than ever with Hero. With Hero, you can easily share your lists with friends, family, and co-workers with just a few clicks. You can create Spaces, which are collections of lists!
To start, sign in to Hero and select the space you want to share. When the space opens, you can share it on social media, through email, or by text message. Once you click “share” on the top-right of the page, your space will be copied to your clipboard!
With Hero, you can easily share and make your lists public in just a few clicks. Whether you're sending a shopping list to a friend or posting your upcoming projects to the internet, you can trust Hero to make it simple and secure.

Creating a Simple Links List

Hero is the ultimate list maker app that allows you to easily create lists of all types, from simple lists of links to full-fledged wishlists.
Creating a simple links list is a breeze with Hero. All you need to do is open the app, select 'Create a Space'. From there, you can easily add any links to the list. You can also add tags, notes, and descriptions to each link, making it easy to organize and find the links you are searching for. You can even add our Hero Chrome Extension to add links you find while surfing the web!
To make your links list even more organized, you can create sub-lists and drag and drop your links to order them. This way, you can easily keep track of all the different kinds of links you have in your list.

Why Hero is the Best List App: Features & Tools of Online List Creator

Here are some of the few reasons Hero is the best app to make and share lists:

1. Create Unlimited Lists & Pages.

Some apps and websites ask you to subscribe to different tiers, with each tier being more expensive. Hero lets you create an unlimited number of spaces for free!
Not only can you make an unlimited amount of Spaces, but in each space, you can create multiple lists (or pages) to display the content you want to share! This is great for categorization, topical pages, and organization when sharing your content with an audience, friends or family.

2. Add your team or friends to build your lists and spaces.

In Hero, you can add collaborators to a Space and build it together. This is helpful if you have a team of researchers or a group of people and want each member to focus on a specific task; one does the research, the other works on the presentation, and another on the graphics!
You can also build travel lists, wishlists, and anything you can imagine together!

3. Community Suggestions.

If you don't want to make someone a collaborator, you can ask them to "Suggest an Item"! When you send someone, or an audience, a link to your public Space, they can "Suggest" items for your approval. Once they suggest an item you approve, they will be credited as the original contributor. This is great if you want to collect a tonne of information from friends; links to the best places they've visited, suggestions for future restaurants, etc.

4. Share your Lists with Pretty URLs.

Some existing list apps restrict the sharing of lists within their app, whereas with Hero, you can share a link with anyone! They don't need an account or the app! The best part is we don't randomize the URL you're sharing, we keep it in line with your brand. You are 100% in control of what your URL will look like, making sharing your Space links easy and memorable.

5. Build Knowledge-bases.

Users on Hero may assume the platform is just for making lists, but individuals have created full-blown community spaces, like this Space for Cybersecurity cheatsheets.

How do I make a list shareable?

To make your list shareable, click the "Share" button on the top right of the screen. This will copy the shareable link to your clipboard, and allow you to send it to anyone!
If you'd like to undo this and make a list private, click the "Sharing" option and toggle "Shareable".
